THE PROCESS OF EXPANDING OUR IT STAFF



1) Consultation

Identifying goals and getting help from industry specialists is key to creating a successful software development team. Knowing your project needs helps determine team growth strategies and lay a strong foundation for adding IT workers.

2) Choice of Team

After gathering requirements, we can help identify necessary team shortages and provide suitable candidates for various roles, including business analysts and software architects. These candidates will blend well with your team.

3) Team Embedding

Once the makeup of your team is determined, we can work on onboarding efforts to help new employees quickly adapt to their new workplace. An initial knowledge transfer session and establishing communication channels can facilitate team building.

4) Leadership

Some clients prefer to manage large teams like internal staff, while others want a designated project or account manager to serve as the primary point of communication. In either case, the client is in charge of the larger team and can monitor its functionality, workload, and communicate with professionals about technical issues.
